Warrior Playstyle

Warrior playstyle is plain and simple.

This list is priority order for rotation

  1. Keep Battle Shout up at all times !
  2. Bloodthirst on CD
  3. Whirlwind on CD. If 3-4 mobs use before BT
  4. Heroic Strikewhile above 60 rage
  5. Cleave when 2 mobs and >60 rage

Using Weakauras like this will help you with managing your CDs and rage.
