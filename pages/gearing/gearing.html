Warrior 60 gearing guide

Warrior gearing once you get 60 is pretty much plain and simple but can be very time consuming.

While being one of the most powerfull classes in all aspects of Vanilla game, a lot of people are playing warriors, so expect competition in every dungeon and raid while playing a warrior.

Here are some weapon suggestions i think yous should consider for your char. Please refer to links below.

  • Dal'Rends Swords Set very powerfull pre-raid swords.
  • Felstriker pre raid bis off hand weapon.
  • Teebu's Blazing Longsword Very good, but very rare weapon.

Getting gear for your Warrior may be rough, boring and time consuming, but it's worth in the end!
